    Our Story

    PhishNet was founded in 2023 by a team of cybersecurity experts and AI researchers who witnessed firsthand the devastating impact of phishing attacks on individuals and businesses. After years of working in enterprise security, we realized that traditional solutions weren't keeping pace with the sophistication of modern phishing campaigns.

    We set out to build a solution that would be accessible to everyone—from individuals protecting their personal information to enterprises safeguarding their operations. By combining advanced machine learning with real-time threat intelligence, we created a platform that not only detects threats but learns and adapts with every scan.

    Today, PhishNet protects thousands of users worldwide, analyzing millions of emails and URLs every month. But we're just getting started. We're committed to staying at the forefront of phishing detection technology and making the internet a safer place for everyone.
